Corporate and Executive Coaching

Working with C-Suite and other senior level executives in Fortune 500 and 100 companies, has led Julie to design and implement programs to promote organizational sustainability, peak performance and meaningful communication. She audits existing programs and organizational structures to identify leaders, streamline communications, nurture cohesive values and build sustainable leadership structures.

Leadership Development

Julie works with clients to both identify and grow leaders. She works cohesively to develop meaningful integration of cultural, cognitive and psychological components for leaders to thrive and interact with success.

Individual Clients

Meeting people where they are and identifying needs are strong areas of interest in Julie’s personal practice. Clients come to Julie for her non-sense approach and her deep intuition. She works directly with clients to set goals, and supports them in moving towards success.

Who Are We

Julie brings 25 years of rich experience to her coaching practice. Viewing life through wide eyes has brought remarkable opportunities and growth beyond expectation. Having built a successful consulting business over the past 20 years, Julie is a leader in her field.

Through her steady management and encouraging hand, she has been witness to the possibilities of others, nurturing space for them to expand their own dreams. Julie has fostered the growth of countless leaders. In her coaching practice, Julie serves as a beacon of truth for others to find and exercise their own power. She works with clients across a broad spectrum to access their values and examine their perspectives to best serve their goals.

A fierce advocate and compassionate listener, Julie serves as a catalyst to help clients find their power and purpose to realize their greatest potential.
